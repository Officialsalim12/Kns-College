Programme Summary

Digital Literacy with IC3 GS6 Certification is designed to provide learners with essential digital skills needed for success in today's technology-driven world. This programme covers seven key domains of digital literacy, ensuring you develop the foundational knowledge required for academic, professional, and personal success in the digital age.

At KNS College, we understand that digital literacy is no longer optional, it's essential. This programme prepares you with globally recognized certifications that validate your digital skills, making you more competitive in the job market and better prepared for further education. With flexible online and hybrid learning options, you can develop these critical skills at your own pace while earning credentials that are recognized worldwide.

Technology Certifications

In today's digital economy, certifications validate your skills and demonstrate your commitment to professional development. KNS College integrates industry-recognized certification preparation directly into your curriculum, ensuring you graduate with both theoretical knowledge and practical credentials that employers value.

IC3 GS6 Level 1 Certification

The Internet and Computing Core Certification (IC3) Global Standard 6 (GS6) is a globally recognized credential that validates your digital literacy skills. This certification serves as a foundation for advanced IT certifications and demonstrates your proficiency in essential digital competencies.

Why Digital Literacy Certification Matters:

Digital literacy is the foundation for success in virtually every career path. Whether you're entering the workforce, advancing your education, or seeking to improve your personal productivity, IC3 GS6 certification provides the credential that proves your digital competence to employers and educational institutions worldwide.

At KNS College, we've made certification accessible and affordable by including exam preparation, exam vouchers, and digital badges in your tuition. You'll graduate with both your certificate and the IC3 GS6 Level 1 certification, giving you a competitive edge in the job market.

Competency-Based Education

Competency: Demonstrated knowledge, skill, or ability required to advance in a certificate program.

Learn at Your Own Pace
Our competency-based approach allows you to progress through the material as quickly as you can demonstrate mastery. Spend extra time on challenging topics and move quickly through areas where you already have experience. This ensures you build confidence and competence before advancing to more complex concepts.

Globally Recognized Certifications

IC3 GS6 is recognized worldwide as the benchmark for digital literacy certification. This credential opens doors to educational and career opportunities across the globe.

International Recognition & Benefits

International Standard: IC3 GS6 is recognized worldwide as a benchmark for digital literacy, making your credentials valuable wherever your career takes you.

Educational Credit: The Level 1 certification is evaluated for 1 semester hour of college credit by the American Council on Education (ACE) in the United States, giving you a head start on further education.

Stackable Credentials: IC3 includes three stackable certification levels (Level 1, 2, and 3) within the GS6 framework, allowing you to progress from foundational to advanced skills at your own pace.

Credential Verification: Earn a verifiable digital badge through Credly that you can share on LinkedIn, resume, and professional profiles to showcase your achievement.

Exam Requirements: Complete and pass the IC3 GS6 Level 1 exam.

Mode of Training: Optional preparation courses are available through many educational providers. Self-study or instructor-led training can be used. Exam can be taken through Certiport test centres or authorised delivery partners.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completing IC3 GS6 Level 1, you must have learnt the following:


Core Digital Knowledge

  • Understand foundational computing terms, hardware, software, and operating system basics.
  • Navigate digital environments and explain basic networking concepts.

Digital Citizenship

  • Create and manage your digital identity.
  • Understand privacy, reputation, and responsible behavior in digital spaces.

Information & Content Skills

  • Perform effective online searches using criteria and tools.
  • Create simple digital content such as documents and presentations.

Communication & Collaboration

  • Communicate effectively through digital tools.
  • Recognise collaborative technologies and digital etiquette.

Safety & Security Awareness

  • Identify common digital threats and basic protection measures.
  • Practice safe online behavior and data protection.

Programme Structure

IC3 GS6 is structured across three levels, each building on the previous:

Level Focus Skills Measured
Level 1 Foundational Basic digital concepts across the 7 domains.
Level 2 Intermediate Working knowledge and application of digital tools.
Level 3 Advanced Advanced problem-solving, collaboration, and technical reasoning skills.

Each Level corresponds to an individual exam and certificate, stackable towards the IC3 Digital Literacy Master Certification for completing all three levels. This programme focuses on Level 1, providing the foundation for advanced digital literacy skills.
